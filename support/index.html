LinkSheet Pro — Support & FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions. Need a hand? Email support@cropsettle.com.

Where can I download LinkSheet Pro?

LinkSheet Pro is available on the Microsoft Store, the Mac App Store, and Gumroad.

What versions of Excel does it work with?

LinkSheet Pro works with both .xlsx and .xlsm (macro-enabled) files — and no Microsoft Excel installation is required for either. It edits your workbook directly, leaving pivot tables, charts, and macros fully intact.

What are the system requirements?

Windows 10 or later, or macOS 12.0 (Monterey) or later. No Microsoft Excel installation is required.

Will LinkSheet Pro modify my original file?

Yes — but a timestamped backup is created automatically before any changes are made, in a folder called LinkSheet Backups next to your Excel file. Your original is always recoverable.

My hyperlinks stopped working. Why?

Hyperlinks point to specific file locations. If you move, rename, or delete the linked files, the links break. Run LinkSheet Pro again on the new folder to refresh them.

What languages does LinkSheet Pro support?

English, Spanish, German, Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, Japanese, and Brazilian Portuguese. The interface follows your Windows or macOS language setting.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by the store you bought from:

· Microsoft Store: request via account.microsoft.com → Order history
· Mac App Store: request via reportaproblem.apple.com
· Gumroad: reply to your purchase email or contact support@cropsettle.com

I need help — how do I contact support?

Email support@cropsettle.com. Please include your operating system, the version of LinkSheet Pro, and a description of what's happening.

Contact: support@cropsettle.com
