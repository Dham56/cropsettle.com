Stop pasting links by hand

LinkSheet for Google Sheets is a Google Workspace add-on that turns the values in your sheet into clickable links to the matching files in a Google Drive folder — automatically. No formulas, no regular expressions, no setup. Because the links are Drive URLs, they keep working for everyone you share the sheet with.

One-click linking

Point it at a Drive folder; every matching cell becomes a link to the right file.

Exact & partial match

Links cells even when filenames don't exactly match — ambiguous and duplicate names are reported, never guessed.

Safe by design

Skips formula cells and pivot output, never double-links, and reads only file names — never your file contents.

Shareable

Drive-URL links work for everyone you share the sheet with, on any device.

How it works

  1. Open Extensions → LinkSheet for Google Sheets → Open LinkSheet.
  2. Choose the Google Drive folder that holds your files.
  3. (Optional) turn on Partial match; pick which tabs to link.
  4. Click Create Hyperlinks — done.
