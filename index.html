Support & FAQ

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Where can I download LinkSheet?

LinkSheet is available on the Microsoft Store, the Mac App Store, and Gumroad.

What versions of Excel does it work with?

LinkSheet works with .xlsx and .xlsm files. Macro-enabled .xlsm files require Microsoft Excel to be installed on your computer. .xlsx files do not.

What are the system requirements?

Windows 10 or later, or macOS. Microsoft Excel is only required for .xlsm macro-enabled workbooks.

Will LinkSheet modify my original file?

Yes — but a timestamped backup is created automatically before any changes are made, in a folder called LinkSheet Backups next to your Excel file. Your original is always recoverable.

My hyperlinks stopped working. Why?

Hyperlinks point to specific file locations. If you move, rename, or delete the linked files, the links break. Run LinkSheet again on the new folder to refresh them.

What languages does LinkSheet support?

English, Spanish, German, Chinese, and Japanese. The interface follows your Windows or macOS language setting.
